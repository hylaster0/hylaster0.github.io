Rules FAQ

Rules Clarifications for cards from Defenders of Neopia as well as the Starry Acara & Fire Uni starter decks.

Jhudora Discards

When Jhudora's Cursed Sword is discarded by the effect of a card, you cannot play it if you are not at least level 3. You must meet the level requirement.

However, this ruling applies to playing cards. If you discard Jhudora's Potion below Level 4, you do the damage.
