Forbidden & Limited List

Banned Cards - You may not include any copies of the following cards in your deck.

Naia, Giant Spyder Web

Limited Brush Types - You may only include 1 Neopet of each of the following brush types in your Rainbow Pool.

Burlap, Chocolate, Mosaic, Robot, Sponge

To clarify, if you run "Chocolate Jubjub", you may not run any other "Chocolate" pets, but may still run any 1 "Sponge" pet in your Rainbow Pool.

Limited Cards - You may only include 1 copy of each Limited card in your deck.

Count Boris

Darigan's Orb

Faerie Hot Dog

Giant Spyders

Lab Ray Scientist

Lady Frostbite

Nilo

Zombified Peasants
