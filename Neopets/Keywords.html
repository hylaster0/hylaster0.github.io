Keywords

Bury - Place the specified cards face-down in your discard pile. Buried cards are unaffected by card effects that do not mention them (e.g. you may not target or recycle them.)

Destroy - Send the destroyed card to the Discard Pile.

Exhume - Turn a face-down card in your Discard Pile face-up.

Enter the Shadows - This Neopet cannot attack, be attacked, or be affected by status effects until the start of their owner's next turn. During that turn, that Neopet deals double damage during the Battle Phase (after subtracting successful blocks).

Fetch - Put the fetching card to the zone it is fetching from, then search for and add the fetched card to your hand.

Foresee X - Look at the top X cards of your deck. You may place them back on the top and/or bottom in any order.

Recycle X - Return X cards from your Discard Pile to the bottom of the deck in any order.

Scrap X - Send the top X cards of your deck to the Discard Pile.

Steal - Place a card face down in your stolen pile. This pile persists between rounds and cannot be interacted with (e.g. Khan the Unstoppable will not remove them). Please return stolen cards to your opponent after the game.
