Song: As God Says (Tatami Galaxy ED) | Arist: Junji Ishiwatari & Yoshinori Sunahara + Etsuko Yakushimaru

About This Song

The ending theme for The Tatami Galaxy, which depicts the singer initially claiming piety and blaming their issues on others before finally admitting the problem lies within themselves. It's a great song for a great show!
