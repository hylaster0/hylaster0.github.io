Song: Falling More In Love (Dan Dan Suki Ni Naru) | Arist: 40MeterP

About This Song

Dan Dan Hayaku Naru is a popular song, but it's actually part of a whole series. This one is probably my favorite, since it's super cute. The singer has a lil (or big) crush on their friend, but can't find it to confess.
