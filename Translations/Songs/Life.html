Song: Life | Arist: FujiFabric

About This Song

Life is the 2nd OP for the anime Silver Spoon and is a cheery, upbeat song reminding its listeners that life is best with friends. It takes a lot of effort to put yourself out there, but your efforts will lead to a whole new world!

We don't have any covers for this song, but you can try following along with the original animal OP!
