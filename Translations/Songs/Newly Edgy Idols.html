Song: Newly Edgy Idols | Arist: More More Jump

About This Song

Note: I've provided a partial translation. This is the portion of the song that shows up in IdolM@ster.
The title of the song probably gives it away, but this song is meant as an introductory "theme song" for (an imaginary?) idol group. (I'm not familiar enough with the series to know if this group has other songs.)
