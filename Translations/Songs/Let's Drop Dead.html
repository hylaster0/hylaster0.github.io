Song: Let's Drop Dead | Arist: Neru

About This Song

A song about someone desperate for attention and praise, despite not being willing to put in the effort or take responsibility for their actions. One thing people seem to overlook is that this song is actually making fun of the protagonist. If you think "wow, they're just like me FR", the artist is actually insulting you.

Covers

An in-house cover by Aster can be found below. Aster is not a singer, though, so prepare your eardrums. You can also find the original version.
