Song: The World Hasn't Even Started Yet | Arist: Pinnochio-P

About This Song

In Pinnochio-P style, this song gives the perspective of a singer discussing the inevitability of death and the problems they inflict on themselves. This one has a bit of a more upbeat message, though, implying they'll come around, make a perspective shift, and find good times.
