Song: V O I C E | Artist: Cloud

About This Song

Yu-Gi-Oh's first openening is all about the struggle to connect with others. The song touches on the inability to keep up with the overwhelming amount of media and pop culture consuming our lives--a message even more relevant now than back in the 90s when it was written. The song's central story tells about the singer's inability to connect with the someone close to them, unable to break past emotional barriers. The song also heavily hints that the singer themselves is depressed, and the person they wish to connect with is in fact trying the same. However, each person's attempts falls on deaf ears, giving the song a tragic end.
