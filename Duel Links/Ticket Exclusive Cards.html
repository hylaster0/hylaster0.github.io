This page details various active tickets in Duel Links. Many cards from tickets are available from the Card Trader or as drops from Legendary Duelists; these cards will be listed separately for each ticket and are generally not recommended.

SR Ticket (App Install Appreciation)

The 5 cards found here, but not in Mission Circuit tickets, are Arcana Force III - The Empress, Lionhearted Locomotive, Lucius the Shadow Vassal, Lyla, Twilightsworn Enchantress, and Machine Angel Absolute Ritual..

I do not recommend any of these cards. Instead, focus on the recommended cards in the "Shared Cards" section.

SR Ticket (Mission Circuit)

Fairy Wind and Garbage Lord are the 2 ticket-exclusive cards that see some play.

Other useful cards are Raidraptor - Pain Lanius, Raidraptor - Vanishing Lanius, Silverokket Dragon, and Trickstar Fusion. Note that you can also obtain these cards as Gate Drops, Level Up Rewards, etc.

Generally Useless Cards: Athena, Attribute Chameleon, Bearblocker, Blizzard Falcon, Cubic Causality, Cupid Serve, Cupid Volley, Divine Dragon Apocralyph, DNA Transplant, Flint Cragger, Fossil Tusker, Howling Insect, Magic Hand, Magicat, Metabo Globster, Nimble Momonga, Rebuildeer, Reload, The Grand Spellbook Tower, The Universal Force, Torrential Reborn, Trickstar Light Arena, Winged Kuriboh
