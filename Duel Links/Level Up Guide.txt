<h1>How To Level Up?</h1>
<p><b>Save limited resources (e.g. gate keys, Duel Orbs), wait for a "1.5x EXP Campaign", which happens roughly 1 week each month.</b></p>
<p>(1) Auto Duel Against Standard Duelists. Increasing your Stage will refresh the standard duelist count. Reach Stage 26 in DM World to obtain 10 Standard Duelist slots. If an event is going on, use your Duel Orbs to refresh the Standard Duelists as well.</p>
<p>(2) Manually duel against a Level 10 Gate Duelist @ 3x Rewards. Picking Level 10 gives you the most XP per gate key, and 3x Rewards speeds up the process significantly.</p>
<p>(2b) Recently, PvE Gate Duelists can be farmed via Auto Duel. Once you've gotten the rewards for the event, focus again on Level 10 @ 3x Rewards.
<p>(3) PvP matches also award XP. Events like Legacy Duels will provide bonus XP for your active character.
