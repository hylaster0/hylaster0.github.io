Farigirafriends

Farigiraf's One-derful Rumble powers up for each Stage 1 Pokémon in play. Utilize powerful support like Cinccino and Revavroom to get set up, then utilize Zoroark's Phantom Transformation to turn into anything you need. With every Pokémon in the format providing 1 prize card, Slowbro's Twilight Inspiration can secure an easy comeback victory!

Rapid Strike Box

Jumpluff's Fluffy Barrage allows it to attack twice, and we've loaded up on special tool cards to let it pump out even more damage! Octillery keeps our deck running smoothly, capable of searching out anything from energy to draw support. Plusle and Minun showcase one of our special exceptions, allowing Plusle to utilize Spark Duo despite Minun sharing the Lightning type.

Pikaclone Quartet

Sometimes, The Pokémon Company will print cards explicitly designed to go together. This deck requires attacking in a very specific order, but can fire off devastating spread damage once the combo comes together. Hawlucha and Raichu pick up the slack for anything that sticks around after. With a combination of Canceling Cologne and Counter Catcher, this combo can even blow through Manaphy's Wave Veil!
