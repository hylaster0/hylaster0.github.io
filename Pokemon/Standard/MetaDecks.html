Dragapult ex

$55

Champions League Fukuoka 7th Place - Yuki Ito

Basic Strategy

Use Budew to lock the opponent from using Items while you set up Dragapult ex on the bench. Then, use Meowth ex to get Team Rocket's Petrel, which searches for Unfair Stamp to shuffle their hand away. From this position, use Dragapult ex's Phantom Dive and Munkidori's Adrena-Brain to place damage counters and set up a 4+ prize turn.

Mega Lucario ex

$35

Champions League Fukuoka 16th Place - Kairu Ishii

Basic Strategy

Set up Lunatone and Solrock to draw cards early, potentially KOing a Basic with Solrock's Cosmic Beam. Then, use Mega Lucario ex's Mega Brave to take consecutive OHKOs boosted by Premium Power Pro. While Mega Brave states it can't be used consecutively, retreating with Switch and Air Balloon will reset the effect. Hariyama's Heave-Ho Catcher can bring in Fighting-weak Pokémon like Meowth ex and Kangaskhan ex to allow OHKOs with Aura Jab paired with Wally's Compassion to heal.
